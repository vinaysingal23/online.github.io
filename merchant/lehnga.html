ONLINE LEHENGA CHOLIS IN MANY AVATARS

Easily the most coveted attire for weddings and festivities, Lehenga Cholis from India are a triumph of cut, craftsmanship and fabric. Often called Ghagra Choli in north and west India, this gorgeous ensemble comprises of a long skirt, a choli that leaves the midriff bare and dupatta. The lehenga designs range from the traditional to contemporary. The Circular Lehenga is traditional and voluminous, and is a bridal favorite. The form-hugging Mermaid Lehenga is loved by bridesmaids for its youthful appeal. Also gaining popularity is the Indo Western Lehenga. The choli is replaced by a long jacket with or without a centre slit, giving it a chic urbane look.

The latest array of Lehenga Cholis online is found at Utsav Fashion, the ethnic fashion portal for India lovers worldwide. If you want to do a spot of online shopping for Lehengas, browse our collection as per your color choice, work and budget.
